Welcome to **jerrygit** — my personal corner of the internet.

I'm Gerardo García, also known as Jerry García in the tech world. The name *jerrygit* comes from *Jerry García IT* — a play on my name and the tools I spend a lot of time with.

## What this blog is about

I work at the intersection of **Telecom, Cloud infrastructure, and AI**. My day-to-day involves:

- Network automation and orchestration (NFV, cloud-native network functions)
- Open Source projects — most notably [Open Source MANO (OSM)](https://osm.etsi.org), where I serve as TSC Chair
- Exploring how AI and LLMs can transform the way we build and operate networks

This blog is where I'll write about all of that — technical deep-dives, project updates, observations, and the occasional opinion.

## What to expect

Posts will be in both English and Spanish. Some will be cross-posted; others might be language-specific depending on the audience I have in mind.

If you're interested in Telco, Cloud, or AI — I think you'll find something useful here.

Let's get started.
